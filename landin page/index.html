Technology

OPTIMIZING LATEST TECHNOLOGY FOR YOUR NECESSITY.

Public Transportation is one of the major necessity for a growing and developing country over the next few years. At the same time, efficient and effective transport systems are critical to sustainability on many fronts, including access to markets, employment, education, health care and other basic services critical to poverty alleviation.

Public Transportation in the Philippines is relatively underdeveloped, partly due to the country's mountainous areas and scattered islands, and partly as a result of the government's persistent underinvestment in the nation's infrastructure. Severity of traffic jams, road quality and infrastructures, accidents, road hazards and weather are all key factors affecting traffic crisis.

Hopndrop is a mobile and web-based app which aims to provide transportation efficiency through an innovative bus management system. The web app offers services to different bus companies such as bus route schedules, and certain pick-up and drop-off points, thus results to obtained compliance on government policies, organized bus lane system, reduced traffic congestion and hassle-free commuting experience.

Experience

ENHANCED USER EXPERIENCE.

Hopndrop mobile app provide commuters an innovative public transportation experience. It is designed for our customers to be able to plan their trip, find their pick-up and drop-off points, see bus route departures in Metro Manila, monitor real-time traffic updates, and be notified of latest news on transport operations. Thus, results to reduced delays, minimize road rage, and of course, efficient travel time management.
